An extension of Coalesence.
Analyzing 5 controversial fonts: Helvetica, Times New Roman, Comic Sans, Papyrus, and Brush Script, and attempting to use them in a new way. My main goal was to have the viewer see these commonly unliked things in a new light. I went on to create a font combining all of the 5 mentioned prior.

Elements: fontself, risograph printer, screenprinting, vinyl, photography, photoshop, illustrator
